About Us

This organization was founded, due to a collective group of mothers who have observed their own sons frustrations and anger, fueled by the injustices around them, that leads to anger, rage and violence. We were concerned that there are not enough positive role models, however we knew a lot of good men, who still held up the principles of provision, protecting their families, communities and country.


A Fact:
68,3 %
of children grow up without a father in South Africa 1

We are here to help

With the help of our sons we identified urgent needs that we could address immediately, like providing toiletries, you might ask why toiletries. The lack of toothpaste, underwear, soap, deodorant and a face cloth are some of the causes that lead to small criminal acts such as shoplifting, theft and later a more serious crimes and violence. The main purpose of this group is to address the immediate need and then deal with more complex issues such as how to navigate life in this ever changing world.

What we do

Every month we invite men to come and have difficult conversations with the boys and provide guidance, we help mentor and counsel and where we can we assist struggling families, specifically those that are child headed.
