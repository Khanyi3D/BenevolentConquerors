Our Initiatives

Tolietry Hamper

When the boys join the program they are given a monthly toiletry hamper with deodorant, lotion and soap.

Mentorship

Exposing the boys to positive role models to build character, resilience, and leadership skills.

Community Support

We provide groceries hamper that provide the basics to child headed households. We also provide much needed renovations to the shacks used as day care center in the informal settlements.

School Shoe Drive

Once a year we provide school shoes to those who are in need.
